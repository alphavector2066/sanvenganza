The Legend

From the red clay of Arandas, a rider returns. He does not gallop in triumph — he arrives in silence, the way a truth long buried resurfaces. The fields of highland agave once knew an unspoken covenant: that what grows slowly in sacred earth, under thin air and fierce sun, must never be corrupted.

That covenant was broken. Additives, colorings, accelerants — the ghost of real tequila filled glasses across the world. San Venganza is the reckoning. A spirit distilled only from 100% certified organic Blue Weber agave, carrying nothing artificial into the bottle. Every drop is a declaration.

"Pure agave. No apologies. This is our vow."

The Expressions

Three Faces of Truth.

Each expression of San Venganza is a chapter in the same story — agave, time, and the uncompromising discipline of craft.

San Venganza

Blanco

Pure · Unaged

Raw agave on the nose. Bright citrus peel, fresh-cut green pepper, white flowers, and a mineral, volcanic earth finish. The land — undisguised and unapologetic. The soul of the agave, unfiltered.

San Venganza

Reposado

Rested · 8 Months in Oak

Vanilla bean, toasted almond, dried apricot, and a whisper of baking spice. Eight months in American oak allow the agave's sweetness to unfold slowly, finding balance without losing its highland edge.

San Venganza

Añejo

Reclaimed · 18 Months in Oak

Dark chocolate, smoked caramel, dried fig, and a long, warm finish of black pepper and roasted agave heart. A spirit for contemplation. The rider's final word — measured, inevitable, and unforgettable.
